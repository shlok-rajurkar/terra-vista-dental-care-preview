Welcome to Terra Vista Dental Care!

Located at the foothills of the San Gabriel Mountains on the northeast corner of Haven Ave and Baseline Rd in Rancho Cucamonga, Terra Vista Dental Care is led by Dr. Sejal Rajurkar DDS. We provide personalized, high-quality dental care for children and adults of all ages, offering everything from preventive treatment to Invisalign Clear Aligners, cosmetic procedures, mercury-free white fillings, and dental veneers.

With over fifteen years of private practice experience and a background in dental research, Dr. Rajurkar practices evidence-based dentistry to achieve the best long-term functional and aesthetic outcomes for her patients.

Our team is carefully selected to be kind, patient, and compassionate — and we hope that comes through from the moment you walk in. We understand that scheduling your first appointment can feel intimidating, but we promise you'll be glad you did! You're also welcome to stop by anytime for a no-strings-attached tour of our newly renovated office — and while you're here, enter our raffle for a chance to win a free professional Zoom teeth whitening treatment (drawn every 3 months). We'd love to meet you!
