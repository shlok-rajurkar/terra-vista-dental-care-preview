We are located at the foothills of the San Gabriel mountains on the northeast corner of Haven Ave and Baseline Rd in Rancho Cucamonga. Led by Dr. Sejal Rajurkar DDS, link to our doctors page we believe in providing personalized, high-quality dental care for all of our patients. We treat children and adults of all ages. Our dental services include a full gamut of procedures ranging from preventive dental treatment to Invisalign Clear Aligners...and everything in between! Ask us about cosmetic procedures such as mercury-free white fillings, dental veneers, and Invisalign clear aligners.

With over fifteen years of private practice experience and a background in dental research, Dr. Rajurkar has mastered the perfect balance in incorporating dental research into clinical dentistry. This allows her to practice evidence-based dentistry and achieve the best long-term functional and esthetic treatment outcomes for her patients.

Our team at Terra Vista Dental Care is carefully selected and trained to ensure that everyone—from the receptionist to the dental assistants—is kind and patient, and treats our patients with compassion and dignity. We are passionate about the work we do and the care we provide, and we hope that passion is evident from the moment you walk through our doors.

We understand that scheduling your first appointment might feel intimidating, but we can assure you that you will be glad you did.
