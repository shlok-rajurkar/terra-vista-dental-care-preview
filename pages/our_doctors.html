Our Doctors

Dr. Sejal Rajurkar brings over 15 years of dental experience and graduated at the top of her class from Loma Linda University School of Dentistry, where she earned a place on the Dean's List.

Her practice centers on patient education and preventive care, with a commitment to honest evaluations and conservative treatment options. Known for her patience, compassion, and famously gentle touch — earning her the nickname "angel hands" — she takes time to truly listen to each patient and doesn't consider treatment complete until both she and the patient are fully satisfied.

Before earning her DDS, Dr. Rajurkar worked as a dental research assistant at the University of Colorado and authored papers presented at the International Association for Dental Research in 2007. She later led a Dental Materials research project presented at both university and state levels.

Whether you're facing a dental emergency or looking to improve your smile, Dr. Rajurkar will guide you through every step with a thorough, personalized treatment plan built around your goals.
