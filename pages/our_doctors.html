Our Doctors

Dr. Sejal Rajurkar has been practicing dentistry for over 15 years. She is a graduate of the prestigious Loma Linda University School of Dentistry, where she finished at the top of her class and was placed on the Dean's List for her academic achievements.

Dr. Rajurkar's primary focus in dentistry is educating patients on oral health and preventive dental care in order to reduce the amount of dental treatment needed over time. She is committed to providing honest and ethical evaluations of her patients' oral condition and always recommends the most conservative treatment options first, while also explaining all available alternatives along with their benefits and drawbacks.

Known for her patience, compassion, and kindness, Dr. Rajurkar is deeply loved by her patients. She has a very gentle touch—so much so that her patients often describe her as having “angel hands.” She takes the time to listen carefully to every concern and obtains a thorough medical and dental history to ensure an accurate diagnosis. Artistically inclined, she strives to bring creativity and precision into her dentistry, particularly in cosmetic treatments. She works closely with her patients and does not consider treatment complete until both she and the patient are fully satisfied with the results.

Before earning her Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree at Loma Linda University, Dr. Rajurkar worked as a Dental Research assistant at the University of Colorado School of Dental Medicine's Research Department. She authored research papers that she presented at the International Association for Dental Research in 2007. Her passion for research continued during dental school, where she served as lead investigator on a Dental Materials research project presented at both University and State levels.

Whether you are experiencing a dental emergency or are unhappy with your smile, Dr. Rajurkar will treat, help and guide you through the entire treatment process. She is very thorough at comprehensively evaluating your oral health and will discuss your short- and long-term goals, and recommend the best treatment plan for you.
